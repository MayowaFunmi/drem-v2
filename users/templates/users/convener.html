...First words from the convener...

Destiny Rescue Mission Int'l (DREM) is a ministry committed to setting captives free. (ISAIAH 61:1-7, LUKE 4:18-19).
One faithful afternoon while on my way to campus ( Christ Apostolic Church Theological Seminary, Lagos State), God gave me the name while on motorbike. I received the calling to be on mission for Christ, to evangelise and groom youths & young adults alike, far back 6 to 7 years ago but was more glaring in the year 2015.
It all started with Facebook page and group, posting of articles by God's grace. Later, God gave directive to start weekly devotionals tagged: PERFECT LAW OF LIBERTY. We received divine instructions to start impacting, grooming & counselling youths via WhatsApp group, which started in 2018 and its tagged: DESTINY DISCOVERY AND COUNSELLING FORUM-DEDACOFOR. And since then, God has really been helping us to go out on outreach to motherless homes, Rehabilitation centres etc.
There are now 3 basic programs we are known with as a ministry according to the measure of grace received:

  1. OUTREACH (to either hospital, rehabilitation centre, prison or motherless home every EASTER MONDAY of the year).
  2. BETHESDA EXPERIENCE (Every October 1st).
  3. OPERATION CARE for the POORS ON the STREET-OCPOS (Every boxing day, December 26th).

MEET THE MAN~~ EVANG. OLANREWAJU DAMILARE AKINYOOLA- O.D.A

Evang. Olanrewaju Damilare Akinyoola is a graduate of computer engineering with honour, bachelor degree holder (2nd class upper) in pastoral theology from prestigious Christ Apostolic Church Theological seminary Ile-Ife (Lagos Campus).
He is an award-winning graduate at Creative Writers Builder Academy as the former Secretary and P.R.O of the School.
He is a storyteller/scriptwriter, an advocate of Godliness amidst youths and young adults, a staunch lover of learning-ever opened to new ideas.

Evang. Olanrewaju Damilare Akinyoola is a scholar, God fearing young man, an astute theologian and visionary leader, a fine academician with brand that cannot be mixed.
A profound thinker and pen writer, an Evangelist with outstanding standards, a sound teacher and results oriented researcher. An energetic minister of God, guest speaker at the different levels of programmes in the vineyard of God.
His ultimate mission/goal is to positively influence his society to better forms, most especially the youths and young adults.
He wants to be part of a cause/movement/corporation that focuses on reforming and that helps in shaping a new way of thinking, so as to aid personality transformation.
