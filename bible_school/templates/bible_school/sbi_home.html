What's meaning of the name SOPHUS?

SOPHUS is a Greek word which connotes 'WISDOM'. It has both male and female name. Sophus is the Male name while Sophia is for female name, both meaning WISDOM in English Language.
1 Corinthians 1:22-24KJV
22: For the Jews require a sign, and the Greeks seek after wisdom:
23: But we preach Christ crucified, unto the Jews a stumblingblock, and unto the Greeks foolishness;
24: But unto them which are called, both Jews and Greeks, Christ the power of God, and the wisdom of God.

As the world revolves round, we are now in a digital age and God has made everything beautify for His own glory. So therefore, as Christians we must always align, update and upgrade ourselves to the various forms of technology.
