WELCOME TO SOPHUS BIBLE INSTITUTE(S.B.I)

INSTRUCTIONS FOR APPLICATION


Please follow the instructions carefully before filling the application form

  1. Click on "Register" in the navigation bar to register on this site. If you have already registered and have username and password, skip this step.
  2. Once registered, Click "Login" in the navigation bar to login to the site using your username and password.
  3. Click on "Bible Institute" in the navigation bar to go to Sophus Bible Institute Page
  4. Read the coordinator's welcome note. Scroll down and click on the "Application" Button. You will then be asked to enter your Application login code.
  5. Enter your Login code and click "Submit" button. You will be redirected to the Bible Institute Application Form.
  6. Fill the form appropriately and submit.
    Note: In the Application form, the last box to check is "Obedience", to indicate your willingness to obey the rules and regulations pf the Institute.
    Anyone who doesn't check the box will have his/her form rejected.
  7. Upon successful submission of your form, you will be redirected to the page showing you the summary of your application details.
